The Problem

Men have been plagued with a rumpled, disheveled look for the past two decades. Well dressed men are the exception, not the rule. That classic, truly American look sported by Ivy League students, professors and dignitaries has been lost to the eons - until now.

Made in China goods wouldn't do for JFK & the Dead Poets Society, so why should it do for you? But the age of cart-delivered catalogues and gifts have long since passed.

Enter Preppy Goodness, Delivered. Straight from the websites, catalogues and salespersons mouths, every week. But not to your mailbox - oh no! instead to your inbox, as befitting a 21st-century gentleman.
