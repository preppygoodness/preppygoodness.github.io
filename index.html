Preppy Goodness, Delivered (for brevity, PGD) is an ideal world in which inboxes (and wardrobes) are filled with the wonder of good style, refined speech and most importantly, preppy gentlemen of all ages. Men have been plagued with a rumpled, disheveled look for the past two decades. Well dressed men are the exception, not the rule. Let's make that the rule.
